About J&W Foods


JW Foods is a leader in quality prepared meals


J & W Foods started as a distributor and wholesale/retail butcher shop providing natural premium graded meats. In 2008, they became an Ontario Inspected Meat Plant. As the business grew and evolved, their passion and love for high quality meats and foods was reflected more and more in their store. They soon became known as a one-stop-shop for health and nutrition. They have gained a solid reputation for providing high quality Chicken that is air chilled, grain fed, and raised locally in Ontario without the use of hormones and antibiotics, as well as, Beef (with some being raised without the use of hormones and antibiotics), Pork (all raised without the use of hormones and steroids) and Fish & Seafood (a mix of wild and farmed raised), high quality frozen fruits and vegetables, healthy & clean dressings and sauces, supplements & vitamins and sports nutrition products.

Our Mission


Our mission is to provide our customers with the best and most cost effective prepared foods and quality ingredients.


VISION: Recently we have created a new in-house meal-prep service. Meal Butler was birthed out of a need for high quality cooked & portion controlled meals that can be prepared within minutes. The products are fully cooked, weighed, portioned, and individually flash frozen in BPA free vacuum sealed bags. This service is designed to simplify and help anyone who is interested in creating and/or maintaining a healthy lifestyle. All products are salt and sugar free, with no added oils. Whether you are looking to lose weight, training for a sport or seeking to eat healthy, these convenient and a-la-cart meals are for you. Try the on-line ordering system to simplify the process and save time.
